StoneAge at the World Cafe Live, Philadelphia
 

Welcome to our Web Site! We have recently changed our lineup and are working hard to prepare for early Fall performances. While we are sad to say goodbye to Ruthie, Mark, and Gary, we are fortunate to replace these outstanding people with new vocalists and musicians who provide a different kind of amazing talent They add a new vitality and shimmer to the band. This Stone Age configuration looks forward to playing the kind of classic rock, soul, blues and country mix that has satisfied our loyal fans and are sure to bring many new fans into the fold. Stay tuned and we will keep you posted as to future gigs. Check out our Band page for photos and brief introductions of band members old and new. Stone Age remains solidly committed to our musical vision: "Heart and Soul and Rock and Roll!" See you soon!

 

For more information about our availability and future plans, or just to have a friendly echat, email us at info@stoneagerock.us.
